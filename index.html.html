❓ FAQ

Frequently Ask
Questions

How do I get access to Momentum AI? +
Register a live trading account with Asia Future Trading (AFT). Once your account is active with a minimum deposit of $100, your Momentum AI access is automatically unlocked. No separate subscription needed.
How do the MAI trading signals work? +
Our analysts post live signals directly through the platform with full details including entry zone, stop loss, take profit levels, timeframe, and confidence score. The MAI Scanner also runs independent AI analysis to validate each signal before it reaches you.
What is the Smart Tool and how does it help me trade? +
The Smart Tool connects your MT5 account to Momentum AI and automates your trade management. Once configured, it handles DCA entries, partial closes at take profit levels, trailing stops, and breakeven management automatically based on your saved settings.
Can I use Momentum AI without connecting my MT5 account? +
Yes. You can access all signals, the MAI Scanner, Trading Journal, News Calendar, and Academy without connecting MT5. MT5 connection is only required for automated trade execution via the Smart Tool.
Is my MT5 account and personal data secure? +
Yes. Your MT5 credentials are encrypted before being stored and are never saved in plain text. All data is transmitted securely and stored on enterprise-grade infrastructure. You can disconnect your MT5 account at any time from the app.
